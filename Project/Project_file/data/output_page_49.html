RPA Consultant in International Consulting Firm

Roles/Responsibilities

  • Help Clients solve their complex business needs through the use of RPA
  • Design efficient and scalable RPA solutions tailored to clients' specific needs using industry-leading RPA tools.

  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including business analysts, developers, and project managers, to ensure successful project delivery.
  • Stay up to date with new industry trends, technologies, and best practices
